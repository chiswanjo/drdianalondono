About Dr. Diana

Medicine With
Heart & Purpose

My mission is to provide you with compassionate care that you can actually understand. I believe in simple language, not fancy words no one can decipher. And I believe we work together — as a team — in English or in Spanish.

I am a Board Certified general urologist who treats a wide array of urological conditions. Beyond medicine, I am a Certified Pranic Healer — because I know that true healing integrates the body, mind, and spirit.

I was recognized with the Physician Leadership Award 2022 by the Los Angeles County Medical Association and have been featured in City of Hope and Latino Leaders Magazine. Physician burnout is real — and when doctors are well, patients are better cared for. We are #StrongerTogether.
